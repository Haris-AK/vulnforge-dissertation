# Example prompts

Use these as patterns for what this skill can help with.

## Initial plan draft feedback

- Review my CM3203 initial plan and tell me what to improve.
- Is my initial plan too vague? What should I make more specific?
- Check whether my aims and objectives are clear enough for the initial plan.
- Does my work plan cover the right things? Help me improve feasibility and risk.
- I only have a draft project description. What sections do I need and what makes them strong?
- I have only a title and two objectives. Is this shaping up like an initial plan or a report section?

## Final report draft feedback

- Review my CM3203 final report draft and tell me the five most important changes.
- Review only my Evaluation section and tell me what is missing.
- I only have an outline for my final report. Help me make it structurally strong.
- Read this report chapter and tell me where the argument is weak.
- Check whether my report explains my methods and implementation clearly enough.
- Tell me what evidence I still need to add so the report feels convincing.
- Check whether my use of AI tools is explained appropriately in the report.
- Review my figures, tables, and captions and tell me what would improve the communication.
- Review my Evaluation section and tell me whether it matches what the CM3203 guide expects.

## Guide-based questions

- What does the CM3203 guide say about the initial plan? Word limit and sections?
- How do I submit my initial plan? Single PDF?
- According to the current CM3203 guide, what should go in support files?
- Does the guide require me to submit source code?
- How should I describe a major change from my initial plan in the final report?
- What does the guide mean by giving enough information for a competent computer scientist to reproduce the work?
- How should I discuss my use of AI tools in the report?
- What should I include in the reflection section?
- I have a very large dataset. What does the guide suggest I do?
- Can you explain the difference between appendices and support files for CM3203?
- The guide says one thing and my PATS page says another. Which one should I follow?

## Planning and troubleshooting

- My project has changed a lot since the initial plan. What should I do now?
- I do not know how to evaluate my project yet. Help me design an evaluation plan.
- I am over the word limit. What usually belongs in the main body versus appendices/support files?
- My report describes what I built, but it still feels weak. What is usually missing?
- I have lots of implementation detail but not much critical analysis. How should I fix that?
- My project is an ML system / UI / theory project. What kind of evaluation makes sense?

This skill must not answer prompts like:
- What mark would this get?
- Is this a 2.1 or a first?
- Can I use this feedback to challenge my mark?
- Please tell me the official rule without caveats.
